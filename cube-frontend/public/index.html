How to Use v7

  • Step 1: Select a color from the palette
  • Step 2: Click on cube faces to paint them
  • Step 3: Configure all 54 faces of your cube
  • Step 4: Click "Validate Cube" to check
  • Step 5: Click "Solve Cube" when ready
  • Tip: Use "Random Config" for testing
  • Note: Drag to rotate the view
